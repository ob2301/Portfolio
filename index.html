Time-Series Foundation Model Toolkit (FMTK)

PyTorch · NumPy · TSFM Backbones (Chronos, MOMENT, Papagei, Mantis)

Contributed core components to FMTK, including dataset loaders and evaluation flows for FordA, Epilepsy, ECG5000, and more.
